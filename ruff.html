My Work

Ruff n Ready

A dog walker app where users are able to find walkers for their dogs while away from home. Throughout this process, I learned to create an app for both android and iOS mobile environments. Conducting user research had helped me develop the app correctly in both environments.

Problem Statement

With more people owning more dogs and the demand in busy schedules, balancing a healthy life for both dog and owner can be difficult. With Ruff n Ready, you are able to book walkers to keep your pup active and healthy while maintaining your day to day schedules. Easy access to contacting walkers and being able to track the location of their pups. Giving opportunities for users who have a love for animals and would like to work walking dogs of all types.

Purpose

Giving opportunities for work and options for anybody who owns a dog that needs to be walked while maintaining a busy schedule.

My Role

UX and UI designer

Gathering Requirements

Who is the user? The user is someone who owns a dog and would like to hire someone to walk their dog vice versa, such as someone who is looking for work as a dog walker.
What major goals and tasks will users need/want to accomplish? Finding a walker nearby to walk their dogs Easy communication with walker and owner, using chat or call Able to track where the walker is with their dog Easy to schedule
What will the product do? Providing users to search for walkers to walk their dog Walkers finding owners who need their dog walked
Where will the product be used? On PC and mobile devices
Why does the user need to use this product? Easy and safe to use, letting the user able to know where the dog is being walked and taken home, while owner is away or at work.
Which platform and devices will the product live on? Computer and mobile devices

User Testing

Scenario:You are a owner who wants to find a walker for your dog.

Number of Participants: 4

Task Given to Users:

Two users are given iOS prototypes to test on the flow of creating an account as a owner and looking for a walker. Inputs on what they like and don't like about the screens. The other two are given android screens with the same task.

Issues: User questioned if there is a call function in case of emergency.

Changes: Added a phone icon in chat window.

Issues: User noticed that there was an arrow next to 'Age' and not on next screen.

Changes: Removed the arrow next to Age options.

Issues: User confused on alert message.

Changes: Reworded the alert message for more clarity.

Issues: User noticed the search box was empty and unclear what it was.

Changes: Added search icon and word 'Search.'
